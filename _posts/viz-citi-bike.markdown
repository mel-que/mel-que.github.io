description_2: "The first dashboard is an overall picture of the number of trips taken throughout 2017. The second dashboard looks at 3 different categories (starting ride times, user and gender type, and generations). Starting ride times chart shows the distribution and trends of trips throughout each day by hour. The user and gender type table shows the distribution of the trips following these dimensions. The generations chart compares number of trips distributed between generations for each month. By looking at these distributions, a viewer can see how the Citi Bike program is performing. More rides are taken during the summer to early fall, or when the weather is warm. There is good consistency with when people ride a Citi Bike during the weekdays (peaks are at 8am and at 5pm – the start and end of a workday) and during weekends (peaks fell between early to late afternoon). Millennials rode the most trips, followed by Generation Y. There are more annual subscribers than 24-hr and 3-day customers combined and male riders biked more trips than female riders in 2017."
