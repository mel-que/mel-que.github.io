description_1: Vegetation survival in subtropical climate can be impacted by prolonged cold weather. A day is considered freezing if the minimum temperature is less than or equal to 0 degrees Celsius. The Enhanced Vegetation Index (EVI) is a satellite-derived measurement that quantifies vegetation greenness (higher EVI values represent more greenness). It is anticipated that more freezing days per year would decrease EVI.
description_2: The visual depicts that there is overall no strong correlation between number of freezing days and EVI values.
